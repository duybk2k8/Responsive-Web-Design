Vietnam Pho Recipe

Pho is a well-known traditional dish in both Vietnam and worldwide by its delicious taste and complicated steps. Today we will break into specific steps to finish this popular dish.

Ingredients

  • 2–3 kg beef bones (marrow and knuckle bones work well)
  • 500 g beef brisket or chuck
  • 1 large onion, halved
  • 1 piece ginger (about 10 cm), split lengthwise
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 star anise pods
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 black caradom pod (optional but traditional)
  • 3-4 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1-2 tbsp rock sugar or regular sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 5-6 liters water

Instructions

  1. Clean and blanch the bones
    • Place bones in a large pot.
    • Cover with cold water and bring to a boil for 10 minutes.
    • Drain and rinse bones thoroughly.
    • Wash the pot to remove impurities.
  2. Char the onion and ginger
    • Place onion and ginger directly over a gas flame, under a broiler, or in a dry skillet.
    • Cook until the surfaces are blackened in spots.
    • Rinse off loose burnt bits.
  3. Toast the spices
    • In a dry pan, toast cinnamon, star anise, coriander seeds, cloves, and cardamom for 2–3 minutes.
    • Cook until fragrant.
  4. Simmer the broth
    • Return bones to the clean pot.
    • Add 5–6 liters water.
    • Add charred onion, ginger, and toasted spices.
    • Bring to a gentle simmer.
    • Skim foam regularly during the first hour.
  5. Add the brisket
    • After about 1 hour, add the brisket.
    • Simmer until tender (about 1.5–2 hours).
    • Remove brisket and cool.
    • Slice thinly for serving.
  6. Continue simmering
    • Simmer the broth gently for 4–6 hours total.
    • Avoid a rolling boil, which can make the broth cloudy.
  7. Season
    • Strain the broth.
    • Add fish sauce, sugar, and salt.
    • Adjust until the broth tastes rich, savory, and slightly sweet.
  8. Prepare noodles
    • Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.
    • Drain and rinse briefly with warm water.
  9. Assemble the bowls
    • Place noodles in serving bowls.
    • Add sliced brisket and/or thin raw beef slices.
    • Ladle boiling-hot broth over the meat (the hot broth cooks the raw slices).
    • Top with green onions and cilantro.
  10. Serve with: Bean sprouts, Thai basil, Lime wedges, Chili peppers, Hoisin sauce and chili sauce (optional)
