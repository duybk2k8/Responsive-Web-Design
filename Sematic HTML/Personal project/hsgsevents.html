Upcoming Events

The Visa 2026

The Visa is the official welcoming event for new students of the High School for Gifted Students (HSGS). Designed to introduce freshmen to the school's vibrant community, the event offers opportunities to connect with peers, learn from senior students, and explore the unique culture and traditions of HSGS.

Mid-Autumn Night 2026

Mid-Autumn Night (Đêm Trăng Tự Nhiên) is an annual festival event at HSGS celebrating the traditional Vietnamese Mid-Autumn Festival with performances, games, and student activities in a joyful and festive atmosphere.

Past Events

NST 2026

Nắng Sân Trường 2026 is a school-wide annual event organized by students of the High School for Gifted Students, VNU University of Science (HSGS). The event celebrates the final moments of the school year, bringing together students across grades through music performances, interactive activities, and meaningful reflections on their school journey. Set in the familiar atmosphere of the school campus, “Nắng Sân Trường” is a place where memories are created, friendships are strengthened, and emotions of youth are shared under the warm sunlight of the schoolyard.

HSGS Cup 2026

HSGS Cup 2026 is an annual football tournament at the High School for Gifted Students (HSGS), VNU University of Science. The event gathers student teams across grades to compete, promote teamwork, and celebrate school spirit in a lively and energetic atmosphere.
